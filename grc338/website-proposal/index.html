Introduction

High Voltage

High Voltage is a family owned cafe where you can kickstart your day with electrifying food and drink. Our coffee is purchased from local businesses and our food is bought by local produce so everything is static fresh and made to boost your energy.

Target audience

Our target audience is working adults and those looking for a great way to start their morning.

The people using this site are the same audience, anyone who is looking for their morning coffee or a place to have a leisurely morning breakfast. The primary reason for visiting the site is to know about our menu, location, history, and about how our food and drink is made fresh and purchased from local businesses.

About

High Voltage has been locally and family owned and operated since 2022. Our goal is to energize all who walk through our door! Whether you are cruising through SLO, coming back from a surf from Morro, or visiting our paradise town… We want you to feel electric!
